Executive Summary

Technical perimeter defenses are increasingly bypassed through Social Engineering. Over 90% of successful data breaches begin with a targeted phishing attack.

To mitigate this human-element vulnerability, we deployed a secure, self-hosted instance of the Gophish Framework to evaluate and train our organizational "human firewall".

Importance

  • Validates Human Defenses: Transforms passive training into active, measurable behavior modification.
  • Identifies Vulnerable Areas: Pinpoints high-risk groups or departments needing targeted educational support.
  • Reduces Breach Risks: Lowers susceptibility to credential harvesting, malware, and financial scams.
  • Tests Incident Response: Evaluates user threat-reporting speeds to optimize internal SOC detection times.
  • Ensures Compliance: Satisfies mandatory awareness training requirements for ISO 27001, HIPAA, and PCI-DSS.
  • Aligns with NIST CSF: Specifically supports the PROTECT pillar by deploying proactive safeguards and training to limit potential attack impact.
  • Optimized Resource Allocation: Enables the district to meet national security standards and protect sensitive student/staff data with zero licensing costs.

Philosophy & Tracking

Real-Time Telemetry

Granular tracking of the attack kill-chain:

  • Delivery: Email routed successfully.
  • Engagement: Open rates via tracking pixels.
  • Interaction: Link click events logged.
  • Compromise: Credential submission tracking.

Business-Friendly

Marketed explicitly as an "Open-Source Phishing Framework" designed to help organizations effortlessly measure employee vulnerability, rather than acting as a complex, aggressive hacker tool.

Non-Destructive Testing

Unlike offensive Red Team tools that steal active session tokens or deploy actual malware, Gophish safely captures behavioral metrics. This ensures that organizational security training does not inadvertently create real security risks.

Deployment Specs

Hardware (100 - 5k Targets)

  • CPU: 1 vCPU (Highly efficient Go binary)
  • RAM: 1GB – 2GB
  • Storage: 10GB – 20GB (SQLite Database)
  • Network: Ports 80/443 (Web) & 3333 (Admin)

Platform: Linux OS

While available for Windows/macOS, Linux is the industry standard for production due to background persistence (Systemd), robust network security (UFW), and minimal OS overhead.

Cost-to-Value Ratio

Because the Gophish engine has such a minimal resource footprint, the entire SOC simulation infrastructure can run on a budget-friendly VPS instance. This provides enterprise-grade analytics at a fraction of commercial scaling costs.
